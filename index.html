About

Spotify is overwhelmed by AI-generated music. Tracks produced by AI tools are being uploaded to the platform faster than it can handle, flooding recommendations, showing up in Discover Weekly, and ending up saved in people's libraries without them knowing.

Spotify calls this content PFC, or Programmatically Functional Content. They are aware it exists. At the moment, they have not been able to give users a way to filter it out.

RealSongCheck is a Chrome extension that fills that gap. Install it, open Spotify in your browser, and it scans your library against our blocklist of confirmed AI artists. Anything that matches gets removed.

How we know what's AI

We maintain a blocklist of thousands of confirmed AI artist accounts on Spotify, updated daily. On top of that, we automatically identify artists who distribute exclusively through AI music generation platforms, and flag accounts with release rates no human artist could sustain.

The detection will miss things. New AI accounts are created faster than anyone can track them. What we catch covers a large proportion of what is currently live on the platform.

Why a Chrome extension

A Chrome extension is currently the only way we are permitted to offer this service. If that changes, we will upgrade accordingly.
